WTIpress

WTIpress is a plugin for WordPress. It lets you translate posts and pages using the WebTranslateit.com service, and makes your WordPress blog multilingual in a few clicks.

>

Installation

  1. Get an account at WebTranslateit.com. It’s a powerful web-based translation tool.
  2. Download the latest release at WordPress.org
  3. Unzip it, and place it in the wp-content/plugins folder of your blog.
  4. Browse the plugins section of your blog, activate the plugin, done.

How does this work?

Synchronize your blog with WebTranslateit.com using the API key. Push the posts or pages to translate. Translate your posts using WebTranslateIt.com. Your blog in English is visible at your usual blog address: www.example.com Your blog in French is visible at www.example.com/fr
